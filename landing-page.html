Overview

Inclusive Jobs’ is a unique online job site and recruitment service dedicated to bringing job offervvet support, Manchester Homelessness Partnership and being part of Greater Manchester’s’ efforts to end and prevent homelessness by bringing candidates to employers who would like to make their recruitment processes, workforce more inclusive. We only work with business's that share our commitment to this and who are opening to looking at recruitment differntly.

If you are looking for work and are unsure if this is for you - jsut ask! More information is on our candidates' page or please get in touch and answer any questions you might have.

See the support options available to help you along the candidate journey to full employement such as help with ID, bank accounts, etc.

If you are an employer who would like to post jobs on our site, please read the employers page.

The services we offer include:

  • Bringing more jobs to people who have experienced barriers to employment and homelessness (or at risk of)

  • 'Facilitating the 'candidate journey' and helping to gain employment opportunities

  • Working with businesses to make their recruitment process more inclusive

  • Bringing more training, volunteering opportunities to our candidates

  • Sharing jobs & other opportunities with organisations who support our candidates

  • Awareness-raising sessions to help businesses better understand homelessness and inclusivity in employment
