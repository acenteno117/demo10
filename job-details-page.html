Job Description

If you are a highly motivated and detail-oriented person who is good at problem solving and wants an opportunity to work in a team environment, then join our Corporate Applications development team! We have an opening for a Software Engineer on an IT team that supports various internal customers. This is a great opportunity to work on a team experienced in following Agile SCRUM methodology, help to migrate our infrastructure to MVC, Service Oriented Architecture, and use Open Source framework including Ruby on Rails. This team is responsible for developing and supporting multiple internal services that are critical to daily activities of various groups within CareerBuilder.


We are seeking a self-directed and flexible Software Engineer with excellent communication skills who will design and implement solutions in .NET or other Open Source framework tools to solve business problems on a company-wide scale. You will work on the analysis, design and implementation of solutions to meet a multitude of CareerBuilder’s business needs. If you are an industrious, well-natured professional and want to work in an enterprising and stimulating environment, then CareerBuilder may be the right place you!

.NET Developer – Software Engineer (Software Developer)

Job Responsibilities:

As a Software Engineer, you will work with other developers and participate in the architecture and design of user interfaces, business objects and overall infrastructure. You will also communicate openly with team leads and other team members in relation to the status of projects and express your thoughts, accomplishments and roadblocks in daily meetings.

Additional responsibilities for the Software Engineer include:

• Maintaining and creating business objects & web services for an N-tier 24x7 production environment
• Developing web pages and web services
• Creating and maintaining customized software modules
• Ensuring that customer expectations are clear and deadlines are met
• Embracing agile lean development methodology for providing fast and efficient delivery of solutions
• Investigating and resolving customer and platform related issues
• Creating and modifying Tables and Data

TSR ID:001835--

Job Requirements

.NET Developer – Software Engineer (Software Developer)

As a Software Engineer, you must be a tenacious and structured individual who has very good object-oriented knowledge and great communication skills. You must also show leadership skills and strive to address problems proactively, methodically and candidly.

Additional requirements of the Software Engineer include:
* Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Computer Systems or other related field
* Familiarity with VB.NET, SQL, Javascript, AJAX, Web Services, ASP.NET, HTTP and/or XML
* Experience developing frontend web pages is a plus
* Experience using Open Source tools like Ruby on Rails is a plus
